Sky Clock

A Simple Clock for Events in Sky: Children of the Light

Stay perfectly in sync with Sky's world events – no matter your time zone.
Sky Clock shows you exactly when each in-game activity starts in your local time, with live countdowns and accurate "Sky Mean Time" conversion (Pacific Time).

What Sky Clock Does:

  • View real-time Sky server time (Sky Mean Time) alongside your local time
  • Track all recurring events like:
    • 🕯️ Grandma's Dinner, Geyser, Turtle Boat, and more candle events
    • 🌀 Shard Eruptions, Rainbows, Fireworks, and seasonal shows
    • 🔄 Daily/weekly resets and rotating shop items
  • Plan your play session with live countdowns to every event
  • No timezone math, no guessing — just clear, accurate timing
